About Me

I'm a people-person with a background in Human Resources who recently turned my full-time focus to web design and development. Over the years I have become obsessed with the magic that can be created with a computer and I love turning ideas and sketches into working products. Formerly an NYC dweller, I've now ventured back to my roots in the Southwest and when I'm not creating or learning new design concepts I'm likely globetrotting, exploring a National Park or cuddling whatever animal is closest.


I would love to help you bring your new business to life or revamp your tried-and-true website!

My Skills

I have attended the Institute of Code web development intensive course and have taken numerous courses through Udemy, Udacity Skillcrush and FreeCodeCamp. I am very dedicated to improving my skills and I continue to take classes and learn new design skills and techniques that make my website coding beautiful and efficient.

Web Development

I hand-code all of my sites and am up-to-date with the latest in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. While I do create my own sites, I am also familiar with WordPress and custom themes and can help you get a personalized page set up if you are looking to maintain a website on that platform.

Web Design

In addition to customizing design through CSS, I am experienced with Photoshop and Lightroom and can edit your photos as necessary to create beautiful content. I have also studied UX design and have an understanding of what types of designs create a positive user experience.

Responsive Design

Responsive design is essential as it ensures that your website will be viewed optimally on all devices. I will code your site so that it will be translate seamlessly between computer screens, tablets and mobile phones.

I am happy to build whatever type of site you are looking for, including e-commerice sites, photo galleries and small-business websites and am available to either create a one-time site or continue to help you in the future with any necessary updates.
